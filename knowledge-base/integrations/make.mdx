[Make](https://www.make.com) is an online automation tool that connects your favorite apps, such as Gmail, Slack, Mailchimp, and over 2,000 more. Greip's Make integration allows you to create automated workflows that connect Greip with other apps and services.

## Prerequisites

Before you can integrate Greip with Make, you need to have the following:

- A Greip account with an active subscription
- A Make account with the necessary permissions to create scenarios and install apps

## Installation

To install the Greip Make app, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to the [Account Settings page](https://dashboard.greip.io/settings) from
    the account dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Under the 'Integrations' section, click on the configuration button on the
    right side of the "Make" app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Click on the 'Copy' button to copy your Make integration API Key, enable the
    integration, and finally click on the 'Greip page on Make'.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    You will be redirected to the Make authorization page. Click on the
    'Connect' button and start creating scenarios to automate workflows between
    Greip and other apps and services. And whenever you are asked to enter an
    API Key, paste the one you copied in the previous step (not your Greip API
    Key).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uninstallation

To uninstall the Greip Make app, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to the [Account Settings page](https://dashboard.greip.io/settings) from
    the account dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Under the 'Integrations' section, click on the configuration button on the
    right side of the "Make" app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Toggle the 'Enabled' switch to disable the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
